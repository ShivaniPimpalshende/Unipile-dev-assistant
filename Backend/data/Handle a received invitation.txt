Handle a received invitation
post
https://{subdomain}.unipile.com:{port}/api/v1/users/invite/received/{invitation_id}
Accept or decline a connection invitation.

Path Params
invitation_id
string
required
The invitation ID.

Body Params
LINKEDIN
INSTAGRAM

Go:

	"fmt"

	"net/http"
	"io"
)

	url := "https://api1.unipile.com:13111/api/v1/users/invite/received/invitation_id"

	req.Header.Add("content-type", "application/json")

	defer res.Body.Close()

	fmt.Println(string(body))
}
