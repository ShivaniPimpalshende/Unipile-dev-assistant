COMMON PARAMETERS

Authentication:
- X-API-KEY (required)
- account_id (required for most actions)

LinkedIn:
- social_id: Unique identifier for posts
- text: Message or post content
- comment_id: Used for threaded replies
- mentions: Used to tag users
- cursor: Used for pagination

Search:
- api: classic | sales_navigator | recruiter
- category: people | companies | posts
- keywords
- location
- industry
- network_distance

Pagination:
Cursor-based pagination is used across list endpoints.
